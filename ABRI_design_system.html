Deliverable 2 · Foundations & the verification badge

Trust, made visible.

The reusable foundation every ABRI screen is built from — tokens, core components, and the verification badge that is the product's single most important, most-reused element. Minimal enterprise language: black, white, a disciplined trust-yellow, and warm neutral grey.

01

Color tokens

Yellow is rationed. It appears only on primary actions, verified status, success, and highlights — never as body text (it fails contrast on white, and scarcity is what makes it read as "trust"). Greys are warm-neutral to sit comfortably beside the gold and feel human rather than clinical.

Typography

Manrope — a modern geometric sans — carries the whole UI with strong hierarchy and generous spacing. IBM Plex Mono is reserved for registry data (SSM numbers, dates, IDs) so verified facts read like an official record, not marketing copy.

Borders are 1px hairlines (Grey 200). Shadows appear only on raised surfaces — modals, the NFC card, key CTAs — never as decoration.

04

Buttons

Hierarchy & states

One primary (yellow) action per view — it's the eye's anchor and must stay scarce. "Save contact" is ink, not yellow, because on the NFC landing it's the everyday action, while the rare, valuable act (verify / join) earns the yellow.

06

The verification badge — the signature element

Three tiers, one system. The badge is the most-reused component in ABRI and the visual embodiment of "trust is the product." Each tier means something specific and is earned a specific way — so the mark is never decorative.

  • 1Circular seal — evokes an official certification stamp; flat, no gloss. Tier sets the fill.
  • 2Inset ring — 1px inner stroke at 60% tier colour, the only "border" the mark carries.
  • 3Glyph — outline, consistent stroke (registry doc → check → star), sized to 60% of the mark.
  • 4Mark sizes — 18 / 38 / 84px for inline / profile / hero. Stroke scales with size.
  • 5Colour rule — Registered = grey, Verified = yellow, Trusted = ink+yellow. Yellow only ever earns its place here and on primary actions.
